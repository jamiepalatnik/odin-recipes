Mum’s salad

Description

My mum is great at making salad. Here's a simple formula for one of the salads she makes.

Ingredients

  • Chopped romaine
  • Chopped yellow peppers
  • Chopped carrots
  • Chopped cucumber
  • Croutons
  • Poppyseed dressing

Steps

  1. Combine the romain, peppers, carrots, and cucumber in a bowl.
  2. Add croutons and a drizzle of dressing on top.
  3. Toss with a fork until the dressing has coated the vegetables.
