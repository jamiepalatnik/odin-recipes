Karen's buttermilk waffles

Description

This is my mum's amazing waffle recipe. We always have it on Christmas morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 2 Tbsp. sugar

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients, then add wet ingredients and mix.
  2. Pour into a well-greased waffle iron and bake until golden.
